Featured Project: Build Your Digital Portfolio

Project Overview

This instructional course walks learners through building a personal digital portfolio using Python/Flask, free platforms like Wix or Notion, and storytelling strategies that connect purpose to design. It’s designed for career pivoters, creatives, and strategic thinkers who want to showcase unfinished work with confidence.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the purpose and impact of digital portfolios
  • Explore platform options (Flask, Wix, Notion, Google Sites)
  • Design a homepage with hero statement and personal branding
  • Organize resume, projects, and contact sections
  • Present unfinished work as part of a growth narrative

Course Format

Self-paced modules with code snippets, templates, and storytelling prompts. Includes downloadable guides and sample layouts.
